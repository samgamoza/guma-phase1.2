Our Story

Cape Town's Neighbourhood Mechanic You Can Actually Trust

Sam's Auto Repair has been a cornerstone of the Cape Town community, delivering honest, high-quality vehicle servicing to drivers across the Western Cape. Founded on a simple belief — that every customer deserves straight talk, fair pricing, and a job done right the first time.

Whether it's a routine service, a complex engine repair, or a last-minute fix before a road trip, our team of skilled technicians handles it all with professionalism and care. We welcome walk-ins and go out of our way to get you back on the road quickly.

What We Do

Comprehensive Auto Repair Services

From routine maintenance to major mechanical work — we've got you covered, whatever you drive.

🔧

Full Vehicle Servicing

Comprehensive scheduled services including oil changes, filter replacements, fluid checks, and manufacturer-recommended maintenance intervals.

🛞

Brakes & Tyres

Brake pad and disc replacements, fluid flushes, tyre fitting, rotation, and balancing. Stay safe on Cape Town's roads.

🔍

Engine Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics to identify and resolve engine faults, warning lights, and performance issues accurately and quickly.

Air Conditioning

Electrical Repairs

Suspension & Steering

Shock absorbers, struts, wheel alignment, ball joints, tie rods — full suspension and steering assessment and repair.
