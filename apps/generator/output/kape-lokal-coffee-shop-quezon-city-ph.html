Our Story

More Than Coffee — It's Community

Kape Lokal was born from a simple dream: to give Quezon City a neighbourhood café that celebrates the rich coffee heritage of the Philippines. Every cup we serve starts its journey in the cool highlands of Benguet and Sagada.

Whether you're catching up with friends, working on a deadline, or just enjoying a quiet moment with a freshly baked pan de sal, our doors — and free WiFi — are always open.
