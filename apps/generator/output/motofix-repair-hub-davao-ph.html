Our Story

Davao-Born, Rider-Owned

MotoFix Repair Hub was built from a passion for motorcycles right here on Quimpo Blvd, Davao City. For over a decade, we've been the go-to shop for riders across Davao del Sur — whether you ride daily, race on weekends, or cruise the highlands.

We stock only genuine and verified quality parts, and every repair is handled by experienced technicians who ride motorcycles themselves. We treat your bike the way we treat our own.

Not satisfied? We stand behind every job with our hassle-free return and redo policy — your satisfaction is our guarantee.
